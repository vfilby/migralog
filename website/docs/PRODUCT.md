# MigraLog - Product Documentation

## Overview
MigraLog is a migraine and chronic pain tracking application designed with simplicity and user control at its core.

## Core Philosophy
**Your data is yours.** Users can export their complete data at any time, maintaining full ownership and control.

## Primary Use Cases

### 1. Pain Episode Tracking
Track migraines and chronic pain episodes with focus on the timeline of events:
- Symptom changes throughout the episode
- Actions taken during the episode
- Timeline-based event logging
- Simple, friction-free data entry (critical when users are in pain)

### 2. Medication Management
- Track medication usage
- Schedule medication reminders
- Log effectiveness within episode timeline

### 3. Medical Analytics (Planned)
- Generate statistics relevant for doctor's appointments
- Visualize patterns and trends
- Export reports for healthcare providers

## Key Differentiators

### Timeline-Focused Tracking
Unlike traditional pain tracking apps that only log start/end times, Migralog captures the evolving nature of pain episodes:
- When symptoms changed
- What interventions were tried
- How symptoms responded over time

### Simplicity-First Design
Recognizing that users are often in pain when using the app:
- Minimal friction in data entry
- Intuitive, frustration-free interface
- Quick access to essential functions

### Data Ownership
- Full data export capability
- Users maintain complete control
- No vendor lock-in

## Features

### Core Features
- Pain episode logging with timeline
- Symptom tracking and changes
- Medication tracking
- Medication reminders
- Data export (full access)

### Planned Features
- Analytics and statistics
- Doctor appointment reports
- Pattern recognition
- Trend visualization

## Target Users
- Migraine sufferers
- Chronic pain patients
- Anyone needing to track pain episodes for medical purposes
- Users who value data ownership and privacy
