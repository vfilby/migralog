## Features

### Feature 1: Timeline-Based Tracking
**Headline:** Track how your pain evolves

**Body:** Most apps only track when pain starts and ends. MigraLog captures the full story—symptom changes, medications taken, relief attempts, and how your body responded throughout the episode.

**Benefit:** Give your doctor the complete picture, not just start and end times.

### Feature 2: Simple When It Matters Most
**Headline:** Designed for when you're in pain

**Body:** We know the last thing you need when you're suffering is a complicated app. Quick logging, clear interface, zero frustration.

**Benefit:** Track what matters without adding to your stress.

### Feature 3: Your Data, Your Control
**Headline:** Your health data belongs to you

**Body:** Export your complete data anytime, in any format you need. No vendor lock-in, no restrictions, no questions asked.

**Benefit:** Take your history to any doctor, any time.

### Feature 4: Medication Management
**Headline:** Never miss a dose

**Body:** Track what medications you take and when. Set reminders so you never miss a dose. See what works within the context of each episode.

**Benefit:** Stay on track with your treatment plan.

### Feature 5: Analytics (Coming Soon)
**Headline:** Statistics that matter

**Body:** Get insights designed for doctor's appointments—frequency, duration, trigger patterns, and medication effectiveness.

**Benefit:** Walk into appointments with confidence and data.
