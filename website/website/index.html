Make sense of your migraines.

A patient-focused, private migraine tracker for iPhone — it turns each attack into a clear timeline, and months of history into something you can actually read.

  • On-device & private
  • No account, no ads
  • Free & open source

      Tracking that keeps up with an attack.

      Four things most trackers get wrong — designed carefully here.

      A timeline for every attack

      Log onset, symptoms, every dose, and relief as the attack unfolds — not one flat entry after it's over.

      Visualizations you'll read

      Frequency, duration, triggers and medication response, charted clearly enough to spot a real pattern.

      Reminders that respect rebound

      Set warning limits on any medication — MigraLog tells you if the next dose is too soon, or you're dosing too often, before it becomes a rebound headache.

      Exports without lock-in

      One tap to CSV, JSON, or a clean PDF for your neurologist. Take your whole history anytime.

      Private by default — and open about it.

      Your data stays on your phone. There's no account, no analytics, and no business model that depends on your symptoms. MigraLog is open source, so anyone can verify exactly that.

      Built after three decades of migraines, by someone frustrated with tools that made tracking hardest exactly when it mattered most.
