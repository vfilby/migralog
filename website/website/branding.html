Brand Identity

MigraLog's visual identity combines the trustworthy calm of purple with the alerting warmth of amber. This pairing reflects our dual purpose: providing supportive care while highlighting important health patterns.

Usage Guidelines

✓ Do

  • • Use purple as primary brand color
  • • Use amber for alerts and important actions
  • • Maintain high contrast for accessibility
  • • Keep designs simple and uncluttered
  • • Use gradients subtly for premium feel

✗ Don't

  • • Use low contrast color combinations
  • • Add visual complexity or clutter
  • • Use bright red (too alarming for pain tracking)
  • • Distort or modify the icon shape
  • • Use small text sizes (min 14px)
