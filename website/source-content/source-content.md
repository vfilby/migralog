# Source content

## Summary

- I have suffered with migraine for the past 3 decades and severe chronic migraine for the past decade.
- I was frustrated with existing solutions that made it difficult to track the details I needed for my doctors and had frustrating edge cases that I didn't want to deal with when I was in pain.
- This is a relatively simple app that contains everything you need to help you get back to that better place.

## Trends and Analytics

![alt text](<Simulator Screenshot - iPhone 17 Pro Max - 2025-11-23 at 18.43.45.png>)

- While we often focus on "migraine days" one of the essential markers is how many days were completely pain-free because even on non-migraine days you can be feeling the after-effects or anxiety about when the pain will start.
- The calendar let's you track what days are completely clear

![alt text](<Simulator Screenshot - iPhone 17 Pro Max - 2025-11-23 at 18.43.57.png>)

- Summary stats that show how many episodes you have had and the distribution of intensity
- How many migraine days
- How many clear, pain-free days
- How much many doses of each medication you have taken

## Non-functional (non-visible)

- Your data is yours.  Everything lives on your device. There are no external servers or databases, just you and your phone.
- No internet? No problem.  No connection needed and everything will work as expected.
- You can export it, all of it.  It's yours afterall.
- Supports dark and light mode so that you don't have to worry about the searing pain of a bright white screen when you are suffering.
- Large buttons, simple UI so that you don't have to fiddle with things when you are in pain.
