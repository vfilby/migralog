1. Brand Overview

Brand Essence

MigraLog is a simple, empowering tool for migraine sufferers to understand their pain today and improve tomorrow. Our brand conveys trust, calm, and optimism.

Navy (#152233)

  • • Trust and stability
  • • Medical professionalism
  • • Calming for anxiety
  • • Gentle on sensitive eyes

Orange (#FF552A)

  • • Energy and warmth
  • • Optimism and action
  • • Forward-looking
  • • Encouraging without stress

Why Navy & Orange?

Our color scheme was carefully chosen to align with our logo while serving migraine sufferers. Navy provides calm and trust without the harshness of bright colors, while orange offers warmth and optimism without being overwhelming during episodes.

Typography Guidelines

  • • Use Navy (#152233) for all headings
  • • Use Gray 900 (#343A40) for body text
  • • Use Gray 600 (#6C757D) for secondary text
  • • Maintain 1.5 line height for body text (1.625 for large text)
  • • Keep line length between 50-75 characters for readability

8. Accessibility

WCAG Compliance

All MigraLog color combinations meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA standards for accessibility.

✓ AAA Rated

Navy on White 16.0:1
White on Navy 16.0:1

✓ AA Rated

Orange on Navy 5.0:1
Orange on White 3.2:1

Focus States

/* Focus States */ *:focus-visible { outline: 3px solid #FF552A; outline-offset: 2px; } /* Form Input Focus */ input:focus { outline: none; border-color: #FF552A; box-shadow: 0 0 0 3px rgba(255, 85, 42, 0.1); }

Accessibility Guidelines

  • • Maintain 44px × 44px minimum touch target size
  • • Use orange (#FF552A) for all focus indicators
  • • Ensure 3px outline with 2px offset for keyboard focus
  • • Orange on white requires large text (18px+) or bold (14px+)
  • • Navy and white combinations are safe for all text sizes
  • • Support system color preferences (prefers-reduced-motion, prefers-contrast)
  • • Test with color blindness simulators (especially protanopia/deuteranopia)
